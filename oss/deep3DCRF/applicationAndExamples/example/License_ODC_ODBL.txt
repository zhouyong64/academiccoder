Attribution:

The data distributed here contain information from the database of the Ischemic Stroke Lesion challenge 2015 (http://www.isles-challenge.org/ISLES2015/).
They were downloaded from (https://www.virtualskeleton.ch/ISLES/Start2015).

Modifications:

The DWI_normalized.nii.gz and Flair_normalized.nii.gz files provided here are modified versions of data from the case "1" of the training set of SISS from the above database. They were modified by Konstantinos Kamnitsas in order to enable the examples of the 3D Dense CRF software.

The images were intensity normalised to a zero-mean, unary-variance within the brain area.
The lesionProbMap.nii.gz is derivative work, produced by method developed utilizing the whole aforementioned database.
